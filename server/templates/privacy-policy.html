Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Introduction

Letterboard SpellerBuddy ("we", "our", or "the App") is a typing education app designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Our Commitment: Letterboard SpellerBuddy is designed with children's privacy as a top priority. We collect minimal data and do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Letterboard SpellerBuddy is designed to work primarily offline. Here is what we collect and store:

  • User Preferences: Settings such as keyboard layout, text size, text color, display name, and avatar selection are stored locally on your device only.
  • Typed Content: Any text typed in the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Google Drive Integration

If you choose to use the optional Google Drive integration:

  • We use Google OAuth to authenticate your Google account.
  • We only access Google Drive to save and retrieve your typed documents.
  • We do not access any other Google services or data.
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time through the app settings.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • The app does not require account creation or login to use core features.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not use tracking or analytics that identify individual users.
  • We do not share any user data with third parties.

Data Storage

All user data, including preferences and typed content, is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not maintain servers that store personal user data.

Text-to-Speech

The text-to-speech feature uses your device's built-in speech synthesis capabilities. No audio or text data is sent to external servers for this feature.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The only optional third-party service is Google Drive for cloud document saving, which requires explicit user action to enable.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data stored on your device. Since data is stored locally, it benefits from your device's built-in security features including encryption and passcode protection.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the app (available through app settings)
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Disconnect Google Drive integration at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
