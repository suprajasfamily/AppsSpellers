# TypeBuddy - Complete Feature List

## Overview
TypeBuddy is a fun, kid-friendly typing assistant app designed for iPad, iPhone, and Android tablets. It helps children learn to type while providing smart tools and extensive customization options.

---

## Typing Experience

### Keyboard Layouts
- **ABC Alphabetical Layout**: 6-row keyboard with letters in A-Z order, perfect for beginners learning the alphabet
- **QWERTY Layout**: Standard keyboard layout for advancing to traditional typing skills
- **Easy Toggle**: Switch between layouts instantly with one tap

### Smart Suggestions
- Context-aware word suggestions that appear as you type
- Kid-friendly vocabulary tailored for young learners
- Tap any suggestion to instantly add the word
- Suggestions update in real-time based on what you're writing

### Customization Options
- **Key Rearrangement**: Drag and drop keys to create your own custom layout
- **Per-Key Sizing**: Long-press any key to resize it (small, medium, or large)
- **Key Spacing**: Choose from tight, normal, or wide spacing between keys
- **Keyboard Size**: Select small, medium, or large keyboard
- **Button Colors**: Pick from 8 vibrant color options for your keys
- **Typing Area Size**: Adjust how much screen space your text takes up

### Typing Helpers
- **Auto-Capitalization**: Automatically capitalizes the first letter of sentences
- **Haptic Feedback**: Feel a gentle tap with every key press
- **Large Delete Button**: Easy-to-find backspace on the right side
- **Space and Enter Keys**: Clearly labeled on the bottom row

## Calculator Mode

### Basic Operations
- Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
- Decimal numbers and parentheses
- Clear and evaluate buttons

### Scientific Functions
- Square root calculations
- Trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan)
- Logarithm function
- Pi constant
- Exponent calculations

### Smart Integration
- Calculator results type directly into your text
- Switch between typing and calculating seamlessly
- Perfect for homework that combines writing and math

## Voice & Audio Features

### Text-to-Speech
- Tap the speaker button to hear your text read aloud
- Stop playback anytime with one tap

### Voice Presets (10 Adult Voices)
- American Man 1 (Deep voice)
- American Man 2 (Standard voice)
- American Woman 1 (Warm voice)
- American Woman 2 (Bright voice)
- Spanish Man (Spanish accent)
- Spanish Woman (Spanish accent)
- British Man (British accent)
- British Woman (British accent)
- Indian Man (Indian accent)
- Indian Woman (Indian accent)

### Reading Speed Control
- Adjustable speed from 0.5x to 2.0x
- Find the perfect pace for your child

### Metronome
- Built-in typing rhythm helper
- Adjustable volume in settings
- Helps develop consistent typing pace
