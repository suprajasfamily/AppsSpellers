## Description

**TypeBuddy is the ultimate typing companion for kids!**

Designed for young learners, TypeBuddy makes typing fun and educational with customizable keyboards, smart word suggestions, and powerful tools that grow with your child.

### Why Parents Love TypeBuddy

**Two Keyboard Layouts**
Start with our beginner-friendly ABC layout where letters are in alphabetical order, then graduate to the standard QWERTY layout when ready. Switch between them anytime with a single tap!

**Smart Word Suggestions**
Our intelligent suggestion bar learns from context and offers kid-friendly vocabulary, helping children write faster while building their word recognition skills.

**Fully Customizable**
Every child is different! Rearrange keys by dragging them, resize individual keys, adjust spacing, and pick from 8 colorful button themes. Create the perfect keyboard for your child's needs.

**Built-in Scientific Calculator**
From simple addition to trigonometry, our calculator handles it all. Results type directly into the text, making homework a breeze.

**Text-to-Speech with 10 Voice Options**
Hear your writing read aloud! Choose from 10 different adult voices including American, British, Spanish, and Indian accents. Adjust reading speed from slow to fast.

**Typing Rhythm Metronome**
Help your child develop a consistent typing pace with our built-in metronome. Adjustable volume lets you find the perfect setting.

**Cloud Saving with Google Drive**
Never lose your work! Save notes directly to Google Drive and access them from any device.

**Safe for Kids**
No ads, no in-app purchases, no social features. Just a focused, safe environment for learning.
